About the Trek

Explore the stunning Sahyadris with our guided trek to Harishchandragad. Experience the thrill of climbing to Konkan Kada, discovering ancient temples like Harishchandreshwar, and camping under the stars. Perfect for solo adventurers, friend groups, and nature lovers.

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Start from Khireshwar village, trek through dense forest, reach top by evening, camp overnight.
  • Day 2: Visit Harishchandreshwar Temple, Konkan Kada and Kedareshwar Cave. Begin descent and return.
